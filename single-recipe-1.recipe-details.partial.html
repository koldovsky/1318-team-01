Ingredients

  • 1 Crumb & Co. French baguette, sliced;
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely minced;
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped;
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped;
  • 1 tbsp fresh oregano, chopped;
  • 4 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil;
  • Salt and pepper to taste;
  • Optional: Grated Parmesan cheese or balsamic glaze for garnish.

Description

Garlic & Herb Bruschetta is a true crowd-pleaser, combining the rustic charm of our freshly baked Crumb & Co. French baguette with a vibrant mix of aromatic herbs and rich garlic. The bread’s crisp texture perfectly complements the savory garlic and fresh herbs, creating a balanced bite that’s light yet satisfying.

Ideal for any occasion, this bruschetta makes a perfect starter for a dinner party, a quick snack, or a flavorful side for soups and salads.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven:Heat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the Baguette: Slice the Crumb & Co. French baguette into 1/2-inch thick pieces and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Make the Garlic Herb Mixture:In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, basil, parsley, oregano, and olive oil. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Brush the Baguette:Using a brush, generously apply the garlic.
  5. Bake:Place the tray in the preheated oven and bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the bread is golden and crispy.
  6. Garnish & Serve:Remove from the oven and optionally garnish with grated Parmesan or a drizzle of balsamic glaze. Serve warm and enjoy!
