Whether you’re interested in placing an order, learning more about our catering services, or just want to ask a question, feel free to reach out. Our team is here to help make your experience as delightful as our baked goods!

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes! We offer a selection of gluten-free and vegan products, including bread, pastries, and desserts. Be sure to ask our staff for the daily options when you visit.

Can I place a custom cake order?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom cakes for all occasions. Simply contact us with your design and flavor preferences, and we’ll work with you to create the perfect cake for your celebration.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide catering for events of all sizes, from corporate meetings to weddings. Our catering menu includes a variety of sandwiches, pastries, desserts, and custom cakes. Contact us to discuss your event needs!

What are your hours of operation?

Our bakery is open from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM on Saturday, and 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM on Sunday.
