About us

Passion for Baking

Welcome to Crumb & Co., artisan bakeries based in the heart of New York City.

Our passion for baking is rooted in the belief that every loaf, and pastry tells a story — one of craftsmanship, tradition, and community. At Crumb & Co., we pride ourselves on using the finest locally-sourced ingredients and time-honored techniques to create breads and pastries that are as beautiful as they are delicious.
Whether you’re stopping by for your morning coffee and croissant, or looking for a special treat to bring home, each bite promises a little taste of New York, with a touch of something special.
We believe in celebrating the artistry of baking, and our team of dedicated bakers and pastry chefs are committed to delivering freshly-baked goods with authenticity and care. Our doors are open to anyone who shares our love for quality and flavor. From our family to yours, welcome to Crumb & Co.—where every crumb counts.
Every Crumb & Co. location is a reflection of its neighborhood, a gathering place where people come together over shared food experiences and simple, flanky, and buttery pleasures.
